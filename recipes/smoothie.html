Pineapple Cleanser Smoothie Recipe

Description

An energizing cucumber and pineapple smoothie with coconut water, ginger, and lemon.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coconut water, or to taste
  • 1 cup chopped fresh pineapple
  • 1 stalk celery
  • ½ cucumber, peeled
  • ½ lemon, peeled
  • ⅓ bunch fresh parsley
  • 1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger root
  1. Blend coconut water, pineapple, celery, cucumber, lemon, parsley, and ginger together in a blender until smooth.
